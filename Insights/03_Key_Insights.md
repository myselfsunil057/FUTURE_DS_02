# 💡 Key Insights

---

### Insight 1: High Churn Structural Risk

Churn rate of **30.70%** indicates a significant retention leak across the platform.

---

### Insight 2: Mobile Experience Weakness

Android and iOS users show higher churn, indicating **mobile UX/performance gaps**.

---

### Insight 3: Buffering = Direct Churn Trigger

Higher buffering time (>4 sec) strongly correlates with user drop-off.

---

### Insight 4: Subscription Tier Impact

Premium users show better retention behavior, validating **upgrade-driven retention strategy**.

---

### Insight 5: Auto-Renewal as Retention Anchor

Users without auto-renewal enabled are significantly more likely to churn.

---

### Insight 6: Network Quality Dependency

Poor network conditions (3G/4G) directly increase churn probability via degraded experience.

---

### Insight 7: Core Demographic Concentration

18–35 age group dominates usage but also contributes the highest churn volume.

---

### Insight 8: Behavioral Signals > Demographics

Churn is driven more by **user behavior and experience quality** than static demographics like gender.

---

### Insight 9: Content Engagement Risk

High-engagement genres (Rock, HipHop) are also potential churn risk zones if content freshness drops.

---

### Insight 10: Predictable Churn Patterns Exist

Churn can be forecast using combined signals: buffering, device type, network quality, and subscription behavior.
